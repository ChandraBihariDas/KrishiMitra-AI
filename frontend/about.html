About KrishiMitra-AI

KrishiMitra-AI is an innovative platform built to empower Indian farmers by combining modern Artificial Intelligence with tried-and-tested agricultural practices. Our focus is to increase productivity, provide actionable insights and make farm decisions data-driven.

We provide features such as crop prediction, pest detection, soil analysis, irrigation advice and weather forecasts so that farmers of all scales can reduce risk and improve returns.

Our team includes agronomists, AI engineers and rural-development specialists working together to deliver localized, affordable and easy-to-use tools for the field.

Whether you are a smallholder or a commercial grower, KrishiMitra-AI aims to be your trusted partner in making farming more productive and sustainable.
